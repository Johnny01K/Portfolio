About me

I’m an artist & web developer. Since art is my passion, I've come to like the idea of creating websites because I can create my own designs and make pages fun to explore and visit. At certain point, I've dipped my foot deeper into the web development, but it feels incomplete. In short, my dream would be making art, design, effects and animations for really passionate studios and series, such as League of Legend's Arcane series, or other spectacular projects

Some other stuff about me... I love video games (obviously), anime, art, protein shakes. I am calm and reasonable, if the moment calls for calm. :)

My knowledge

I'm an MSc in IT – Software Engineering and over the years of uni, I was slowly heading to web development category. Since I had to travel a lot and am a freelancer, having tens of different subjects and different programming languages, I didn't have much time to perfect one specific category and make many projects of my own.

I mostly work with HTML, CSS and JS. I've ran a few courses and worked on some uni projects with React, TypeScript and PHP / Laravel. I do art in Procreate iPad app and I've used Photoshop in the past, but not fully experienced with. I'm also starting to use Aseprite program for pixel art and Blender. Visit pages "Projects" & "Art Gallery" for a better preview of my work.

I am currently working as a Freelancer for a Twitch streamer Skill4ltu for over 3 years. I am part of his Twitch moderator team and I run his Discord server as an admin of over 11k members. These activities require constant and good English communication skills within the chat or server.
